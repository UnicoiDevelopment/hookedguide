App Store Screenshot Guide
==========================

Required device sizes:
- 6.7" iPhone (iPhone 15 Pro Max): 1290 x 2796
- 6.5" iPhone (iPhone 14 Plus): 1284 x 2778
- iPad Pro 12.9" (6th gen): 2048 x 2732

Screenshots to capture (in order):
1. Home screen with weather conditions and fishing rating stars
2. The Guide — species selection grid
3. The Guide — water details form (showing temp, water type, clarity)
4. Guide results with full AI recommendation narrative
5. Rig Builder with complete rig (sweet spot tier)
6. Fish Heat Map with colored zone overlays on MapView
7. Species detail page (e.g., Largemouth Bass)
8. Gear review with "Check Price" affiliate button

Steps:
1. Build to TestFlight: eas build --platform ios --profile production
2. Install on device from TestFlight
3. Take screenshots on each required device size
4. Optionally frame with device bezels using Fastlane Frameit or screenshots.pro
5. Upload to App Store Connect under each device size tab
